Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

This strawberry-banana protein smoothie with almonds and protein powder is great for a meal replacement or after a workout.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Â¼ cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate flavored protein powder

Directions:

  1. Place strawberries, banana, almonds, and water into a blender; blend to combine. Add ice cubes and puree until smooth. Add protein powder and mix until evenly incorporated, about 30 seconds..
